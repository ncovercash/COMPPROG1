Statistics and Usages Policy
============================

From 13 Apr 2014, |J| will collect statistics and usages of |J| to help improve the package features.
Data we have collected are your |J|'s settings and |st| informations.
To disable automatic sending statistics and usages, set ``send_stats_and_usages`` to ``false`` and |J| will not send any statistics and usages anymore.
However, additional packages statistics still collected for packages improvements and selections.
